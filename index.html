Our Story

More Than Brunch

Brunch & Cake was born from a simple but powerful idea: that every hour of the day deserves food that makes you feel good. Nestled in the vibrant Park Street Mall in Sheikh Zayed, our boho-chic venue is a place to slow down, gather with the people you love, and enjoy dishes that are equal parts beautiful and delicious — from our legendary French toast to our showstopping cakes and everything in between.

We open our doors every morning at 9AM and stay open into the late evening, because great food and great company shouldn't be rushed. Our warm, polite team is here to make every visit feel effortless and special — whether it's your first time at our table or your hundredth. Walk-ins are always welcome, and we can't wait to see you.
